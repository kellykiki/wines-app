Rosé of Cabernet Sauvignon

This wine costs 14.0 $ and it is produced at Vinum. Here is what you should know about it: Cabernet Sauvignon may be the hardest variety to make a rosé from because it yields such a big wine. Vinum tries their best, but despite good fruit and acidity, the wine is heavy, lacking the racy vivacity a great blush wine should have.
