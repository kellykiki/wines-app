Rose & Thistle

This wine costs 35.0 $ and it is produced at Adair Bachman. Here is what you should know about it: This is made from 92% Cabernet Sauvignon and 8% Pinot Noir, an interesting and fulfilling combination thats robust on the palate. Pinkish-orange in color, it's both tangy and weighty, finishing dry in orange zest.
